Altai

URL
YouTube

Altai is one of the most beautiful places in Russia. Primarily because of Mountains: if you drive along the ridge, you will see slopes strewn with pine trees, mountain rivers and lakes. And if you open it in the car windows, you can get acquainted with the invisible wonder of these places — mountain air.

The climate in Altai is temperate, so it is best to go here in summer. This way you will see all the diversity of the local flora and fauna. Through the woods Moose roam the Altai, eagles fly over the ridges, and graze on the plains roe deer. And the famous manuls are also inhabitants of the Altai Territory.
